MongoFlow limitations

1. AI-generated queries may be incorrect or inefficient.
2. AI outputs may vary across runs.
3. The AI assistant depends on an external model provider.
4. Schema inference depends on the available sample documents.
5. Users should review AI-generated commands before execution.
6. Sensitive data should not be sent to the AI provider unless privacy requirements are satisfied.
7. The current evaluation was conducted in an educational setting and does not replace long-term industrial validation.
8. Full latency benchmarking and repeated AI-output stability testing remain future work.
